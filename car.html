This 1967 Austin Mini is a left-hand-drive Mk I
This 1967 Austin Mini is a left-hand-drive Mk I model that was acquired by its current owner in 2003 and subsequently refurbished and modified from 2006-2007 at a reported cost of more than $80k. The car was refinished in blue with a black sliding sunroof over red leather upholstery, and power is provided by a rebuilt 1,275cc inline-four with a Cooper S-style camshaft, dual SU carburetors, and a stainless steel exhaust system. Additional features include a four-speed manual transmission with a “magic wand” shifter, an alternator conversion, Minilite wheels, front disc and finned rear drum brakes, and more. This Mini is offered on dealer consignment with a British Motor Industry Heritage Trust certificate, refurbishment documentation, and a clean Oregon title.
